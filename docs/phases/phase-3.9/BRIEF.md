# Phase 3.9: Terminal UX Polish — Brief

## Goal

Turn the terminal from a technically capable renderer into a polished IDE
terminal experience.

## Summary

Phase 3.9 focuses on product quality rather than core emulation depth. After
the renderer and compatibility layers are in place, this phase improves the
user-facing details that make the terminal feel integrated, comfortable, and
worthy of a full IDE.

## Intended Outcome

- Better selection, copy, and paste behavior
- Stronger scrollback experience
- Search and navigation quality improvements
- Cleaner integration with the rest of the IDE's focus and command model
- A more refined terminal experience overall

## Boundaries

- No major backend rewrites
- No large architecture split unless another refactor phase explicitly owns it
- Do not hide emulation gaps with UX polish; correctness still comes first
